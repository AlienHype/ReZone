More About ReZone

At Rezone, our mission is to inspire and empower communities to take action in preserving our beaches and coastal environments. We believe that every small effort makes a significant difference in maintaining the beauty and health of our beaches for future generations.

Inspire and Empower Communities

Encouraging active participation in beach preservation efforts.

Raise Awareness

Sustainable Practices

Promoting environmentally friendly habits, such as reducing plastic use and recycling.

Regular Events

Hosting bi-weekly community meetings to discuss and plan actions for maintaining clean beaches.

Collecting cigarette butts

Preventing cigarette butts from being washed into the ocean, where they can release toxins and harm marine ecosystems.

Weekly Beach Cleanup

Volunteers spread out and pick up litter along the beach. Encourage small groups to focus on specific sections (shoreline, dunes, walkways, etc.).

Sand-Sifting Machines

This machine can filter out particles from the sand while leaving the environment undisturbed.

Planting mangroves and Filao

By planting mangroves and Filao trees together, we can strengthen coastlines, improve local biodiversity, and provide long-term ecological and economic benefits for coastal communities.

Virtual reality (VR) Showcase

3D models and animations to accurately represent marine environments and the effects of human actions.
